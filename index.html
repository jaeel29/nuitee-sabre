About us

Nuitée is more than a wholesale accommodation supplier - we're a tech company that creates web applications for the entire travel distribution chain. Our Core API connects direct hotel suppliers and wholesalers to our global supply, giving us access to hundreds of thousands of hotels and enables us to offer highly competitive rates with real-time availability. 

 

Whether you prefer commissionable rates or Net Rates, we give you control over your pricing. At Nuitée, we believe in providing our clients with flexible payment options that fit their unique needs, whether that's paying immediately via corporate credit card, making a deposit, or opting for weekly payments. Get in touch with us to find out how our customized solutions can take your travel agency to the next level.
